Thank you for your interest in Volunteering

ASHIYANA4ANIMALS CARE FOUNDATION is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting animal welfare through education. Our mission is to cultivate compassion towards animals, empathy for people, and care for the environment among school-going children.

Our enthusiastic team of volunteers and interns forms a growing network of animal lovers who actively support our mission. We recognize the vital role that education plays in fostering a more compassionate society. We firmly believe that addressing animal abuse and mistreatment requires fundamental changes in societal attitudes towards animals and the environment.

If you share our passion for animals and wish to make a meaningful contribution towards building a compassionate society in India, we invite you to join us as a volunteer or intern. Your involvement will include teaching school children about compassion towards animals, empathy for people, and environmental care. Additionally, you can support us by creating content on humane education topics that may be included in school textbooks endorsed by various state educational boards in India.

As a volunteer or intern at ASHIYANA4ANIMALS CARE FOUNDATION, you can engage in a variety of activities such as :

● Promoting ASHIYANA4ANIMALS CARE FOUNDATION on social media platforms.

● Conducting humane education programs and workshops in schools.

● Organizing workshops for college students on animal welfare and environmental sustainability.

● Assisting at the shelter or participating in foster care for rescue cases in your own home.

● Getting involved in rescue cases directly.

● Contributing to digital marketing efforts.

● Fundraising initiatives.

● Providing transportation assistance for rescue cases.

● Engaging in content writing related to our cause.
