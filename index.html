About Us

ASHIYANA4ANIMALS CARE FOUNDATION is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting animal welfare through education. Our mission is to cultivate compassion towards animals, empathy for people, and care for the environment among school-going children.

Our enthusiastic team of volunteers and interns forms a growing network of animal lovers who actively support our mission. We recognize the vital role that education plays in fostering a more compassionate society.

We firmly believe that addressing animal abuse and mistreatment requires fundamental changes in societal attitudes towards animals and the environment.
