About Us

Against the backdrop of the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020, we started a small initiative of feeding animals, wherever we could find them. Gradually our initiative graduated towards the rescue of stray animals and their rehabilitation.

This small initiative was initially started by a family who is passionate about helping animals. In the beginning, this initiative started with a very small amount of support, be it physical or financial. We struggled a lot in our initial days however we didn’t lose hope. Step by step we gained support and got bigger and better day by day.

Over the last two years, we have gained the trust of many people, and their attitude toward local animals have changed drastically after seeing us.

Initatives

Some of the works that we have carried out since our inception are highlighted below,

● Collar to cows to avoid accidents, brutality and manhandling.

● Rescue of animals, from dogs, cats, horses and cows, to even birds and snakes.

● Provide homes and arrange the adoption of homeless puppies and kittens, while making sure that they are in the right hands.

● During the times of pandemic, the country went into lockdown and humans have shut themselves away from contact with the outside world. It left many street animals without any food, which soon became a worrisome sight. It was then that our NGO came forward and started feeding more than 500 stray animals daily.
